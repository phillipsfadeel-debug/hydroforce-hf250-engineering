# 🔧 HydroForce HF-250 PRO — Industrial Systems Design & Facility Layout

---

## 📋 Project Overview

This repository serves as the comprehensive engineering README and technical specification manual for the end-to-end product industrialization framework, commercial feasibility models, process workflow architectures, and manufacturing facility layout designs for *HydroForce Industries (Pty) Ltd*, based in Epping Industrial, Cape Town.

The primary objective of this enterprise is the local manufacturing and assembly of the *HydroForce HF-250 PRO* — a heavy-duty, SANS-compliant 250 Bar electric pressure washer designed to serve as an agile, cost-effective alternative to premium imported European brands. The engineering framework bridges macroeconomic market strategy with micro-level shop floor workstation ergonomics, systematically structured across the evaluation rubrics of Course *FAM360S*.

### ⚙️ Phase 2: Process Engineering & Macro-Flow Synthesis

- *Production Style Selection:* Implemented a hybrid cellular assembly line and fixed-position layout optimized for batch manufacturing to achieve the targeted production volume growth.

- *Process Mapping:* Generated comprehensive Operation Process Charts (OPC) and Assembly Charts capturing the exact chronological sequence of operations and inspections.

- *Method Study & Logistics Optimization:* Engineered Flow Process Charts (FPC) documenting transport distances and structural delays. Evaluated two alternative Master Flow Patterns using a multi-criteria weighted evaluation matrix scoring safety, material handling effort, and backtracking avoidance.

---

### 🏭 Phase 3: Facility Layout Construction & Work Design

- *Systematic Layout Planning (SLP):* Developed Activity Relationship Charts (ARC) and proximity worksheets to logically arrange production zones relative to auxiliary services (Toolroom, Testing, Offices, and Ablutions).

- *Work Study & Ergonomics:* Designed custom manual assembly workstations using Modapts Standards to calculate baseline standard times, reduce industrial fatigue, and maximize line efficiency.

- *Layout Optimization & Assembly Line Balancing:* Applied balancing algorithms to minimize idle times and eliminate work-in-progress (WIP) accumulation. Formulated strict dimensioned footprint parameters for logistics nodes (Receiving, Warehousing, Shipping) to produce the final 2D blueprint layout.
